Daiquiri

Daiquiri is a simple cocktail drink made up of 3 ingredients
and for a garnish we use a simple lime twist.

Nothing more, nothing less for a simple summer drink.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces or light rum
  • 1 ounce of lime juice
  • 3/4 ounce of symple syrup

Steps:

  1. Add the rum, lime juice and demerara sugar syrup to a shaker with ice, and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled coupe.
  3. Garnish with a lime twist.
