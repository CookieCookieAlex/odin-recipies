Whiskey Sour

Whiskey Sour comes with a quite refreshing taste with only 4 ingredients and a foamy top.
This cocktail doesn't need a finishing touch in my opinion but
you can always put a dry orange wheel or angostura bitter on top for a nice look.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces of burbon
  • 3/4 ounce of lemone juice
  • 1/2 ounce of simple syrup
  • one egg white

Steps:

  1. Add bourbon, lemon juice, simple syrup and egg white, if using, to a shaker and dry-shake for 30 seconds without ice.
  2. Add ice and shake it again until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass.
  4. Garnish with a dry oring wheel.
